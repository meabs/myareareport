Data sources

Police UK Crime API

Street-level crime and stop & search data published by police forces across England, Wales and Northern Ireland. Updated monthly. data.police.uk

Environment Agency Flood Monitoring

Real-time flood warnings, alerts, and river level readings from monitoring stations across England. environment.data.gov.uk

Postcodes.io

Open source UK postcode geocoding and lookup service. postcodes.io

HM Land Registry Price Paid Data

Residential property sale prices in England and Wales, updated monthly. Queried via the Land Registry SPARQL endpoint. landregistry.data.gov.uk

National Highways WebTRIS

Traffic flow monitoring data from motorway and A-road sensors managed by National Highways. Daily vehicle counts and HGV percentages. webtris.highwaysengland.co.uk

GOV.UK Fuel Finder

Live petrol and diesel prices from filling stations across the UK, published under the Motor Fuel Price (Open Data) Regulations 2025. developer.fuel-finder.service.gov.uk

OpenStreetMap

Map tiles © OpenStreetMap contributors, licensed under ODbL. openstreetmap.org

All government data is published under the Open Government Licence v3.0. Crime data is anonymised and location-snapped to the nearest street node.
